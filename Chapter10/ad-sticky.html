amp-ad sticky ads

This page has a sticky ad—it sticks to the bottom of the page, and it's dismissable.

Sticky ads are created by wrapping an amp-ad inside an amp-sticky-ad component, like this: 

     <amp-sticky-ad layout="nodisplay">
       <amp-ad
         width="320"
         height="50"
         type="doubleclick"
         data-slot="/12345678/sticky">
     </amp-ad>
    </amp-sticky-ad>
